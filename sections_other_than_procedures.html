Vascular Audit Data Collection

New admissions are labelled as AAA | Access | Amputation | CEA | LLR | Miscellaneous | Trauma | ULR | Veins or Wound.

The data is collected in each of the following 6 sections :

  1. Mode of Admission i.e. elective/emergency and source
  2. Pre-operative Assessment including risk factor assessment preoperative diagnostic tests and anaesthetic assessment
  3. Details of the procedure(s) performed as well as additional procedures or procedures required to treat complications
  4. Post-operative recovery, length of stay, type of post-operative recovery bed, and discharge whether to step down or usual place of residence
  5. Initial post-operative in hospital complications if any and the outcome of such complications as well as post-operative morbidity and mortality
  6. Long-term follow-up

Section 3, 'Details of the procedure(s)', contains a wide collection of data elements, reflecting the complexity of the relevant surgical procedure.
The other sections contain many data items common to all admissions together with extra data items and coding standards for specific admissions.
